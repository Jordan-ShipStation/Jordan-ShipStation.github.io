A Guide to Using ShipStation

ShipStation is a web-based shipping software that is designed for single channel and multi-channel merchants. Anyone who sells on multiple platforms (Shopify, eBay, Amazon, etc.) & ships physical goods can improve their shipping efficieny to save time, money and grow their business with ShipStation.

In these lessons below, I'll show you how to get started with ShipStation. Follow along and you'll learn how to get set up, ship your orders, and utilize the power of ShipStation to maximize your shipping process.
