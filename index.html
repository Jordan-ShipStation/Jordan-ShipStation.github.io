1. Add a Ship From Location

1. Go to Account Settings.


2. Click Shipping, then Select Ship From Locations.


3. Click the Add a Ship From Location button.



4. Enter the Ship From Address and Return Address Information.



5. Click save changes.

2. Add a Shipping Carrier

1. Go to Account Settings.


2. Click Shipping, then Select Carriers and Fulfillment.


3. Click the Add a Shipping Provider button.



4.Select a Shipping Carrer and Click Connect



5. Follow the On-screen Instructions to complete the Carrier Connection.

3. Connect a Selling Channel

1. Go to Account Settings.


2. Click Selling Channels, then Select Store Setup.


3. Click the + Connect a Store or Marketplace button.



4.Select a Selling Channel or Marketplace you wish to connect.




5. You will see on-screen instructions appear, Follow the steps to complete the connection.

4. Configure Label Layout & Printer Settings

1. Go to Account Settings.


2. Click Printing, then Select Printing Setup.


3. Click the Document Options link next to Labels.



4. The label settings window will open, here Select the Label Layout of whether you'd like a packing slip to print with the label or label only. 



5. Click save changes.
